1 · Completeness

NULL / BLANK SCAN
Counts missing or blank values across every column of a chosen table.

2 · Uniqueness

DUPLICATE SCAN
Groups by a key column to surface duplicate values that should be unique.

3 · Integrity

REFERENTIAL / ORPHAN SCAN
Uses your join keys: rows in the left table with no matching row in the right table.

4 · Conformity

FORMAT / PATTERN SCAN
Checks that values in a column conform to an expected format.

5 · Consistency

CROSS-TABLE COMPARISON
For rows matched on your join key, compares a column from A against a column from B.

6 · Relevancy

COLUMN UTILIZATION SCAN
Flags columns that are mostly empty (below the threshold) — low-relevancy / low-value fields.
